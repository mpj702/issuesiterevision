Stay safe around trains

Every 100 minutes someone is struck by a train. Rail safety is an important issue and needs to be talked about more. There are many ways to be safe around trains. Always watch for the lights and the gates when you are at a crossing. It is always important to listen for the horn as well.

However, some crossings are in quiet zones so engineers may not be able to blow their horns when they come up to a railroad crossing. This is why it is very important to look. It is also important not to climb on railroad equipment. If the train begins to move the slack can roll in and throw a person off. This can lead to serious injury or even death. It is also important to know that railroad property is private property. There are serious consequences such as a fine or even jail time if you are caught trespassing.
